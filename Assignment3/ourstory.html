Our Story

"Budape" or "Budapest", derives its name from the Grand Badapest Hotel film. Our restaurant's design is inspired by the film's Morandi hue. Particularly, our menu reflects a synthesis of cuisines from China, Italy, and beyond, providing you with the opportunity to experience the flavors of many cultures all at one stop.
